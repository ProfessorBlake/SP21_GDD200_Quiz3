Quiz 3


Press SPACE to spawn a ball. Press R to add a force and change the color.

Please remake this scene in Unity. Your game should:
  • Have a ball prefab which has a circle collider and rigidbody2D.
  • Instantiate a ball at a random position when SPACE is pressed.
  • Add the ball to a list when it's created.
  • When R is pressed the balls should all have a force added to them and they should turn red.

To set the color of a sprite renderer to red you can use Color.red.
